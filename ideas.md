### **Approach 1: Minimalist Brutalism**
**Design Movement:** Digital Brutalism with Neo-Brutalist principles

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, unpolished aesthetic celebrating function over form
- Bold typography with high contrast and deliberate white space
- Monochromatic with strategic accent colors (single bold hue)
- Asymmetric layouts that feel intentional, not accidental

**Color Philosophy:**
- Base: Pure white background (`#FFFFFF`) with charcoal text (`#1A1A1A`)
- Accent: Single vibrant color (e.g., electric cyan `#00D9FF` or deep orange `#FF6B35`) used sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Rationale: Stark contrast creates visual tension and emphasizes content hierarchy; the single accent color becomes a signature

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric grid with intentional "broken" alignment
- Hero section: Large typography on left, whitespace on right
- Content blocks: Varied widths (40%, 60%, 100%) creating visual rhythm
- Navigation: Minimal, possibly hidden or bottom-aligned

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Oversized typography**: Display fonts at 64px+ for section titles
2. **Geometric dividers**: Angled lines, asymmetric borders separating sections
3. **Negative space**: Generous padding and margins creating breathing room

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: Color shift to accent (no scale, no blur)
- Click feedback: Instant, no animation (feels snappy, not playful)
- Transitions: 200ms linear (not eased—feels mechanical, intentional)

**Animation:**
- Minimal motion: fade-in on scroll (opacity only, 300ms)
- No parallax or floating elements
- Loading states: Simple line animations or dot sequences

**Typography System:**
- Display: **IBM Plex Mono** (bold, 700) for headings—monospace creates technical feel
- Body: **Inter** (regular, 400) for content—clean and readable
- Hierarchy: 64px → 48px → 32px → 18px → 14px
- All caps for section labels (e.g., "ABOUT ME", "PROJECTS")

### **Approach 2: Glassmorphism with Depth**
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Glassmorphism + Neumorphism fusion

**Core Principles:**
- Layered glass-effect cards with subtle depth and shadow
- Soft, rounded corners and frosted glass aesthetics
- Gradient overlays creating visual hierarchy
- Smooth, fluid animations and micro-interactions

**Color Philosophy:**
- Base gradient: Soft gradient from light blue (`#E3F2FD`) to lavender (`#F3E5F5`)
- Glass cards: Semi-transparent white with backdrop blur (rgba 0.7 opacity)
- Accent: Gradient from teal (`#00BCD4`) to purple (`#9C27B0`)
- Rationale: Layering and transparency create depth; gradients feel modern and premium

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Centered, card-based layout with floating glass panels
- Hero: Full-width gradient background with centered card overlay
- Sections: Staggered card positions creating visual flow
- Navigation: Floating sidebar or top nav with glass effect

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Glass cards**: Semi-transparent containers with blur backdrop
2. **Gradient accents**: Subtle gradients on buttons and highlights
3. **Floating shadows**: Soft, multi-layered shadows creating depth perception

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: Lift effect (slight scale + shadow increase)
- Click feedback: Smooth scale animation (1 → 0.98 → 1)
- Transitions: 300ms cubic-bezier ease for fluid motion

**Animation:**
- Entrance: Fade + slide-up on scroll (300ms)
- Floating elements: Gentle Y-axis movement (±2px) on hover
- Loading: Animated gradient shimmer across cards

**Typography System:**
- Display: **Poppins** (bold, 700) for headings—rounded, modern feel
- Body: **Outfit** (regular, 400) for content—geometric, contemporary
- Hierarchy: 56px → 40px → 28px → 16px → 14px
- Accent text: Gradient text on key phrases

### **Approach 3: Dark Elegant with Neon Accents**
**Design Movement:** Cyberpunk elegance meets premium dark mode

**Core Principles:**
- Dark, sophisticated background with neon accent colors
- High contrast creating visual pop and energy
- Grid-based layout with geometric precision
- Tech-forward aesthetic with premium polish

**Color Philosophy:**
- Base: Deep charcoal (`#0F0F0F`) to near-black (`#1A1A1A`) background
- Foreground: Off-white (`#F5F5F5`) with subtle gray tones
- Neon accents: Cyan (`#00FF88`) and magenta (`#FF006E`) used strategically
- Rationale: Dark background reduces eye strain; neon accents create visual excitement and tech credibility

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Vertical grid-based structure with precise alignment
- Hero: Dark background with neon accent line/border
- Sections: Dark cards with neon borders or glow effects
- Navigation: Sidebar with neon highlights on active states

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Neon borders**: Glowing borders on cards and CTAs (text-shadow glow effect)
2. **Grid lines**: Subtle background grid creating structure
3. **Accent glows**: Neon color glows on hover states

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: Neon glow intensifies, border color shifts
- Click feedback: Pulse animation with neon color
- Transitions: 250ms ease-out (snappy but smooth)

**Animation:**
- Entrance: Fade + neon glow animation on scroll
- Hover: Glow intensifies with color shift
- Loading: Animated neon line scan effect

**Typography System:**
- Display: **Space Grotesk** (bold, 700) for headings—geometric, futuristic
- Body: **Inter** (regular, 400) for content—clean and professional
- Hierarchy: 60px → 44px → 32px → 16px → 13px
- Code blocks: **JetBrains Mono** for technical content
